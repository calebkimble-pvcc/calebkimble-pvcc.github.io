Subtitle I: National Park System

Subtitle I, titled "National Park System," delves into the operational intricacies of the parks themselves. This section encompasses a broad array of topics, including park administration, personnel management, interpretive programs, museum operations, and resource management. It lays out the foundational principles governing the establishment, management, and preservation of national parks, ensuring that these treasured landscapes remain accessible, protected, and enjoyed by present and future generations.
The laws contained within Subtitle I provide the framework for the day-to-day functioning of national parks, outlining the roles and responsibilities of park staff, establishing mechanisms for public engagement and participation, and delineating procedures for the development and implementation of park management plans. Additionally, this subtitle addresses critical issues such as visitor services, educational programs, and the maintenance of park infrastructure, all essential components in fostering the NPS's dual mandate of preservation and public enjoyment.
