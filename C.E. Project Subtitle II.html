Subtitle II: Outdoor Recreation Programs

Subtitle II, titled "Outdoor Recreation Programs," extends beyond the confines of individual parks to encompass broader initiatives aimed at promoting outdoor recreation and conservation nationwide. This section focuses on the coordination of outdoor recreation activities, the enhancement of recreational opportunities on public lands, and the conservation and recovery of natural resources. By fostering collaboration between federal, state, and local entities, Subtitle II seeks to expand access to outdoor recreation while safeguarding the ecological integrity of natural landscapes.
Key provisions within Subtitle II address the development of recreational infrastructure, the provision of grants and assistance for outdoor recreation projects, and the promotion of environmental stewardship and outdoor education. Moreover, this subtitle underscores the interconnectedness of outdoor recreation and conservation, recognizing the intrinsic value of natural landscapes and the importance of preserving them for future generations.
