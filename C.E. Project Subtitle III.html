Subtitle III

Subtitle III, titled "National Preservation Programs," underscores the NPS's commitment to preserving the nation's cultural and historic heritage within the context of national parks. This section focuses on the implementation of preservation programs aimed at safeguarding historic sites, cultural landscapes, and archaeological resources found within park boundaries. It outlines the responsibilities of the NPS in managing and protecting these irreplaceable assets, ensuring their continued relevance and integrity for years to come.
Within Subtitle III, provisions address a wide range of preservation activities, including the identification and documentation of historic resources, the development of preservation plans and guidelines, and the promotion of heritage tourism and public education initiatives. By prioritizing the conservation of cultural heritage, Subtitle III reinforces the NPS's role as a custodian of the nation's collective memory, enriching the visitor experience and fostering a deeper appreciation for the diverse tapestry of American history.
